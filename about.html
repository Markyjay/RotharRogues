Welcome to "The Rothar Rogues"

The Rothar Rogues concept provides a worthwhile weekly outlet for retired men in the Durrow area. The group is focused on leisure cycling as a means of keeping fit, active and healthy, placing a strong emphasis on the leisure aspect of activities. Getting from A to B as slowly as possible is one of the groups mantras!

Relaxing after a cycle

At the Rother Rogues Men's Shed Group, we believe in the power of camaraderie and the importance of creating a supportive environment for men. Our shed provides a welcoming space where you can engage in various activities, such as woodworking, metalworking, gardening, and community projects. Whether you're a seasoned craftsman or a beginner looking to learn new skills, our diverse range of projects caters to all interests and abilities.

Donaghmore bike week

Mental health is a priority for us, and we understand the challenges that men may face in seeking support. Our shed offers a safe space where you can openly discuss your experiences, share your emotions, and connect with others who may be going through similar journeys. We firmly believe that by fostering a sense of purpose, accomplishment, and social connection, we can positively impact mental well-being.

St Patrick himself arrived

The Rother Rogues Men's Shed Group extends beyond the shed itself. We actively engage with the community of Durrow, undertaking projects that benefit the local area and support charitable initiatives. From building community gardens to repairing community facilities, we take pride in our contributions and the positive change we bring to our surroundings.

Handlebars of friendships

We also collaborate with local organizations, schools, and community centers to offer workshops and share our skills with a wider audience. By passing on traditional crafts and practical knowledge, we aim to preserve valuable skills and inspire younger generations.

Spring Cycle

Joining the Rother Rogues Men's Shed Group means becoming part of a tight-knit community where friendships are forged, skills are honed, and well-being is nurtured. Whether you're seeking an escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life or simply looking for a place to connect with like-minded individuals, we invite you to join us and be part of our journey. Come and experience the warmth and support of the Rother Rogues Men's Shed Group. Connect with us today and embark on a fulfilling and enriching journey of self-discovery, skill-building, and fellowship in the heart of Durrow, Ireland.
