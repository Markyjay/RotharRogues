Rothar Rogues

In the picturesque village of Durrow, Ireland, a remarkable initiative known as the Rother Rogues Men's Shed Group has been making a significant impact. Established to provide a space for men to connect, share skills, and promote mental well-being, this community-driven organization has become a beacon of camaraderie and support. This website delves into the inspiring story of the Rother Rogues Men's Shed Group and explore the positive influence it has had on the local community.

Welcome to the Rother Rogues Men's Shed Group, a vibrant community in the heart of Durrow, Ireland. We are a gathering of like-minded individuals who come together to share skills, build connections, and promote mental well-being.
