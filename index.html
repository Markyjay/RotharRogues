Meet "The Rothar Rogues"

In the picturesque village of Durrow, Ireland, a remarkable initiative known as the Rother Rogues Men's Shed Group has been making a significant impact. Established to provide a space for men to connect, share skills, and promote mental well-being, this community-driven organization has become a beacon of camaraderie and support. This website delves into the inspiring story of the Rother Rogues Men's Shed Group and explore the positive influence it has had on the local community.

Welcome to the Rother Rogues Men's Shed Group

"Mens Shed on Wheels"

A vibrant community in the heart of Durrow, Ireland providing a weekly cycling outlet for retired men that includes a leisurely country road cycle, interspersed with the customary coffee breaks, where banter, yarns, storytelling and the occasional poetry reading is exchanged. The group take part in local parades and festivals and organise the occasional tour of cycle Greenways and Blueways of Ireland.

Rothar Rogues origins & Purpose

"The Rothar Rogues concept provides a worthwhile weekly outlet for retired men in the Durrow area. The group is focused on leisure cycling as a means of keeping fit, active and healthy, placing a strong emphasis on the leisure aspect of activities. Getting from A to B as slowly as possible is one of the groups mantras!"
